About NSFriends

Nigeria Scrabble Friends is a players' intuitive reponse birthed to complement the activities of Nigeria Scrabble Federation by providing a relaxed enviroment for players integration, recreation and representation using the tools of social media.
NSFRIENDS started off as a social media response to players' demand for a strong online presence before it metamorphosed into a solid work unit that has been facilitating Scrabble events and competitions.


We started on Facebook in January 2015 with about 50 players. With Nigerian Scrabble players in mind, we set our intentions and laid the rules and it was a vibrant hit
In February, 2015 we started the whatsapp chatroom and there, the game changed. Whatsapp platform redefined us and gave us the impetus to grow and blossom. We have provided room for;

  • Education: Quizzes and study Aids
  • Integration: We are National
  • Competitiveness: We encourage and promote competition chiefly through NSPC and regional meets.
  • Members Support: We are a leaning shoulder for friends in time of need, where we can.
  • Relaxation: We are a fun group


It is our hope that in the years to come we can widen our reach to bring more players to the scrabble fold using the tools available to us and leveraging on the solid support of our friends.
